# Advisory Council Structure

## Roles & Responsibilities

### Founder & Chief Architect (Jawahar R Mallah)
- **Authority**: Supreme Governance Veto, architectural vision, security oversight.
- **Term**: Indefinite.

### Technical Council
- **Members**: Top code contributors and system architects.
- **Responsibility**: Code reviews, performance optimization, and API standards.
- **Selection**: Merit-based appointments by the Founder.

### Legal Oversight Advisor
- **Responsibility**: Ensuring compliance with Indian Law (IT Act, GDPR, etc.) and trademark protection.
- **Focus**: License enforcement and institutional compliance.

### Cultural & Historical Advisory Panel
- **Responsibility**: Guiding the "Cultural Preservation Mandate."
- **Focus**: Ensuring the project respects and promotes diverse heritage.

### Community Representation Board
- **Responsibility**: Voice of the users.
- **Focus**: User experience, accessibility, and community feature requests.

## Governance Framework

### Term Limits
- **Founder**: Indefinite.
- **Technical Council**: 2-year revolving terms.
- **Advisory Panels**: 1-year terms, renewable.

### Decision Authority
- **Security/Core**: Founder (Final decision).
- **Features/UI**: Technical Council + Community Board (Consensus).
- **Legality**: Legal Oversight Advisor (Binding on compliance).

### Conflict Resolution Mechanism
1. **Mediation**: Discussion within the relevant council.
2. **Review**: Advisory Council joint session.
3. **Escalation**: Final adjudication by the Founder.
