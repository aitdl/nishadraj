# Trademark Protection Strategy

## Registration Plan
- **Brand Name**: "NishadRaj"
- **Logo/Branding**: Specific protection for the stylized "NishadRaj" emblem.
- **Classes**:
    - Class 9: Software, operating systems, and digital assets.
    - Class 41: Educational services and technical training.
    - Class 42: Cloud computing, software research, and technology services.

## Licensing Model for Partners
Official partners must sign a **Trademark Use Agreement** to ensure:
- Brand consistency.
- No dilution of the mission.
- Compliance with the Sovereign OS principles.

## Enforcement & Monitoring
- **Unauthorized Use**: Automatic blocking of unauthorized forks using the "NishadRaj OS" name in public registries.
- **Brand Integrity**: Monitoring marketplace platforms for counterfeit or misleading services.
- **Cease & Desist**: Legal action against entities claiming official affiliation without authorization.

## Governance Integration
The trademark is held by the **Trust/Foundation** on behalf of the community, managed by the Advisory Council to prevent private enclosure.
