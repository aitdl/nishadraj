## 1. University Collaboration Structure
NishadRaj OS seeks formal partnerships with universities to advance research in sovereign computing.
- **Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)**: Framework for long-term technical and academic cooperation.
- **Resource Sharing**: Access to NishadRaj OS core architecture for educational and research purposes.
- **Joint Steering Committees**: Co-governed research initiatives focused on digital sovereignty.

## 2. Research Fellow Program
Inviting doctoral and post-doctoral researchers to contribute to the OS ecosystem.
- **Fellowship Tiers**: Junior Fellows (Students), Senior Fellows (Established Scholars), and Distinguished Fellows.
- **Sponsorship**: Grant-based financial support for research aligned with the NishadRaj roadmap.
- **Output**: Fellows are expected to produce original research, code contributions, or architectural analysis.

## 3. Citation & Peer Review Protocol
Maintaining high academic standards for all project-related publications.
- **Citation Standards**: Mandatory citation of NishadRaj OS as the foundational platform.
- **Peer Review**: Internal governance review followed by external academic peer review for all official whitepapers.
- **Open Access**: Encouraging publication in open-access journals to ensure knowledge remains public.

## 4. Archival Digitization Partnership
Collaborating with university libraries and archives to preserve cultural heritage.
- **Digitization Standards**: Using NishadRaj-approved metadata and format standards for archival integrity.
- **Long-term Preservation**: Integrating archived data into the NishadRaj sovereign cloud for civilizational continuity.
- **Access Protocols**: Defining scholarship-based access levels for sensitive historical data.

## 5. Student Internship Model
Nurturing the next generation of sovereign developers.
- **Structured Internships**: 3-6 month programs focused on specific technical or governance modules.
- **Mentorship**: Direct guidance from AITDL architects and senior technical council members.
- **Credentialing**: Formal certificates of completion recognized within the NishadRaj ecosystem.

## 6. Joint Publication Policy
Rules for co-authored scholarly work.
- **Authorship Attribution**: Clear attribution to both NishadRaj OS contributors and university researchers.
- **Review Period**: A 30-day governance review period for any publication using NishadRaj OS internal data.
- **Non-Commercial Mandate**: Ensuring that joint publications serve the public interest and academic knowledge.

## 7. Academic Ethics & Neutrality Clause
Ensuring partnerships remain independent and objective.
- **Non-Partisan Requirement**: Partners must agree to maintain absolute neutrality in their research and public communication.
- **Conflict of Interest**: Mandatory disclosure of any funding or associations that could compromise academic integrity.
- **Freedom of Research**: Protecting the scholar's right to objective technical analysis, even when critical of specific implementations.
