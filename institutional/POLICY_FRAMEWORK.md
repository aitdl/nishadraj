## Code of Conduct
All contributors, members, and participants must adhere to a standard of professional respect, inclusivity, and technical excellence. Harassment or exclusionary behavior is strictly prohibited.

## Governance Modification Protocol
1. **Proposal**: Submitted via formal Governance Request.
2. **Review**: 14-day public review period.
3. **Validation**: Must pass `validator_agent` integrity checks.
4. **Approval**: Founder or Council consensus required.

## Public Transparency Rule
All governance files, task registries, and impact logs MUST be publicly readable. Private repositories for core governance are forbidden.

## Security Disclosure Policy
Vulnerabilities must be reported privately to `security@nishadraj.com`. Disclosure to the public is permitted only after a fix is deployed or 90 days have passed.

## Contributor Ethics Clause
Contributors must disclose any potential conflicts of interest with corporate entities or competing projects. Contributions must be original and free of encumbered IP.
