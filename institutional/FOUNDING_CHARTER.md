## Vision
To build a computing legacy that honors ancestral wisdom while embracing future technology, ensuring that digital tools serve humanity without compromising individual freedom.

## Commitment to Open Knowledge
NishadRaj OS is committed to providing free access to its core architecture, documentation, and educational resources, ensuring that the technology belongs to the people.

## Governance Integrity Pledge
We pledge to maintain the highest standards of integrity. No single entity shall have the power to secretively alter the core laws of the system. All changes must pass through the deterministic validation layer.

## Non-Political Neutrality Clause
NishadRaj OS remains strictly non-political. Its tools and infrastructure shall not be used to propagate partisan political agendas or discriminate against any group.

## Cultural Preservation Mandate
The project shall actively support the digitization and preservation of local culture, languages, and history, ensuring that the digital age does not erode cultural identity.
