## Mission Statement
To establish a sovereign, autonomous, and ethically governed open-source ecosystem that empowers individuals and communities through decentralized technology and open knowledge.

## Governance Philosophy
NishadRaj OS operates on the principle of **Digital Sovereignty**. It is designed to be independent of centralized corporate or political control, governed by deterministic rules (Code as Law) and overseen by a multi-stakeholder advisory council.

## Digital Sovereignty Mandate
- **Data Residency**: All sovereign data must reside within jurisdiction-compliant hosting nodes.
- **Integrity**: Every core component must be signed and verified.
- **Transparency**: All governance decisions and code changes are publicly logged.

## Legal Options

### Option A: Section 8 Company (India)
- **Legal Entity**: A non-profit company registered under the Companies Act, 2013.
- **Objectives**: Promotion of specialized research, education, and digital infrastructure.
- **Advantage**: Professional management structure, ease of receiving grants, and perpetual succession.

### Option B: Public Charitable Trust
- **Legal Entity**: A trust established for charitable purposes, including "advancement of any other object of general public utility."
- **Focus**: Community-centric management, long-term asset protection.
- **Advantage**: Significant tax benefits and simple setup for non-commercial open-source preservation.

## Founder Role: Jawahar R Mallah
- **Position**: Chief Architect & Life-Term Trustee/Director.

## Community Participation Structure
- **Technical Council**: Elected contributors overseeing the codebase.
- **Advocacy Groups**: Local chapters promoting the use of NishadRaj OS.
- **Governance Voting**: Stakeholder-based voting for non-core architectural decisions.
